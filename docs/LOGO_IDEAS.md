# NOTSENT — Logo ideas (concepts only)

**Do not implement until you approve.** These are written concepts for a designer or for briefs; no assets or code have been added.

---

## 1. **Sent / unsent**
- **Idea:** A single envelope or paper plane that’s either “crossed out” or has a subtle “not” (e.g. a diagonal line, or a small “no” badge).
- **Vibe:** Clear, literal “this didn’t get sent.” Works well for app icon and favicon.
- **Risk:** Can look generic if the “unsent” element is too subtle.

---

## 2. **Message bubble + pause**
- **Idea:** One chat bubble with a “pause” or “stop” symbol inside (or a hand/open palm) so it reads as “message held / paused.”
- **Vibe:** Messaging + intervention in one mark. Fits product name and demo.
- **Risk:** Pause icon can read as “mute” or “audio”; needs testing at small sizes.

---

## 3. **Open hand / “hold”**
- **Idea:** Stylized open hand (palm out) as the main mark — “stop,” “hold,” “we’ve got you.”
- **Vibe:** Calm, protective, non-judgmental. Good for a supportive brand.
- **Risk:** Can feel like “stop” or traffic; keep lines soft and minimal so it doesn’t read aggressive.

---

## 4. **Letter “N” + strikethrough**
- **Idea:** Bold “N” for NOTSENT with a horizontal line through it (or through a second “N”) so it reads as “not sent.”
- **Vibe:** Typographic, product-name-first. Works in wordmark and as a compact icon (e.g. “N̶” or “N” in a rounded square).
- **Risk:** Strikethrough can feel negative; consider a softer “fade” or “ghost” treatment instead of a hard line.

---

## 5. **Two states: sent vs not sent**
- **Idea:** Two minimal shapes side by side (e.g. two circles or two bubbles): one “full” or checkmarked (sent), one empty or with an “×” (not sent). NOTSENT is the second state.
- **Vibe:** Clear before/after, “we’re the path where it doesn’t send.”
- **Risk:** Slightly more complex; might need to be simplified for small sizes.

---

## 6. **Breath / pause mark**
- **Idea:** A single curved line (like a breath or a comma) or a small “…” that suggests a pause before sending.
- **Vibe:** Calm, minimal, “take a breath.” Less literal, more emotional.
- **Risk:** May not read as “messaging” or “intervention” without the wordmark.

---

## Recommendation for demo
- For **fast demo/pitch:** Option **4 (N + strikethrough)** or **2 (bubble + pause)** are the most recognizable and easy to describe.
- For **brand warmth:** Option **3 (open hand)** or **6 (breath/pause)** lean into support and calm.

**Colors (already in use):** Dark `#0a0a0a`, accent `#8B5CF6`. Logo can be white/light on dark and purple for accent use.
